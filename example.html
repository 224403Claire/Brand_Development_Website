Our product

One pan wonders

Vantastic offers One Pan Wonders, a cookbook designed for van life, featuring delicious recipes that require just one pan.

It simplifies cooking on the road by highlighting local ingredients, making every trip more memorable through the exploration of regional flavors.

With easy-to-follow recipes using a small number of fresh, local ingredients, One Pan Wonders provides a unique and practical way to cook in a camper van.

Our vision

Our vision is to inspire a world where van life is effortless, freeing, and truly adventurous.

Our values

Journey over destination

Journey over destination means enjoying the trip, not just the places you go. It's about having fun on the road, seeing new things, and making memories along the way. The adventure of traveling in the van is just as important as reaching your final stop.

Adventure made easy 

Adventure Made Easy means that your adventure is easy and stress-free. That you discover new places easily, without having to worry about complicated preparations or obstacles. It is all about the joy of travelling, discovering new things and enjoying the moment, without it having to be difficult.

Going whenever, wherever 

Going whenever, wherever means you can travel anytime you want, and go anywhere you like. You don’t have to follow a strict plan. You can just pick a place, drive, and explore at your own time. It’s all about having the freedom to go wherever and whenever you want.

Something out of nothing 

Something out of nothing with a camper van means turning any place into a home. Whether you’re parked by a lake or in a random spot, the camper van lets you create a cozy, fun experience anywhere. It’s about making the most of what you have and enjoying simple moments wherever you go.
